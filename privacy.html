Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Overview

EverythingNowNow respects your privacy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect limited technical information related to automated video generation and publishing.

2. Data Collected

  • Basic OAuth tokens for TikTok account authorization (no passwords).
  • Technical metadata (file paths, upload status, timestamps).
  • No personal, financial, or location data is collected.

3. Use of Data

Collected data is used solely to generate, upload, and manage short video content through authorized APIs. We do not share data with third parties except when required by law.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our platform does not use cookies or third-party analytics. Standard server logs may be stored for debugging and performance optimization.

5. Third-Party Integrations

EverythingNowNow uses APIs from TikTok, YouTube, and OpenAI. Each integration follows the respective provider’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Security

Access tokens and configuration files are stored securely and never exposed publicly. GitHub and hosting infrastructure use encrypted HTTPS connections.
